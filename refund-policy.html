Refund Policy

Welcome to UndoEngine. We value our customers and want the trial and subscription process to be transparent.

1. Trial Period

  • Each user may access a 5-day free trial.
  • During the trial, users have full access to the features of the chosen subscription (Standard or Premium).
  • To avoid being charged, users may cancel the trial within the 5-day period.

2. Subscription and Payments

  • After the trial ends, the subscription will start automatically.
  • All payments after the trial are non-refundable.
  • Users cannot request a refund for any period after the trial.

3. Cancelling a Subscription

To cancel the trial or subscription, users may:

  • Use the “Manage subscription / Cancel subscription” link provided by Creem in the subscription confirmation email;
  • Or send a request to our email: sfbackupservice@gmail.com.

4. Contact for Questions

If you have any questions regarding the trial, subscription, or refunds, please contact us at: sfbackupservice@gmail.com
